# AtlasEdu AI 🚀

### Transforming Higher Education in Morocco

AtlasEdu AI is an innovative educational platform designed specifically for university students in Morocco. Our mission is to break down academic barriers by utilizing AI to automatically summarize, translate, and clarify university lectures into Moroccan Darija and French.

## What We Do
* **Smart Summaries:** Instantly convert long lecture audio and PDFs into concise, easy-to-read study guides.
* **Localized AI:** Tailored specifically to understand local Moroccan dialects (Darija) and official academic French.
* **Student-First Architecture:** Built on cloud infrastructure to ensure fast, low-latency access for students across Morocco.
